Introduction to Local Area Networks (LANs)

Below is an interactive simulation of a local area network or LAN. The term LAN generally refers to a collection of computers contained within a single geographic location such as a building or room. Although the term implies physical proximity, this is not always the case. LANs are only really logical collections of devices that share common configurations.
